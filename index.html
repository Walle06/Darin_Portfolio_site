Darin Anthony Laing is a Oklahoma born Artist who has spent his life exploring and progressing the boundaries of the arts. From his grass roots doing improv and local theatre in the suburbs of florida to tackling the Big Apple one audition at a time Darin has never relented on his pursuit to bring happiness to people through the mediums of theatre and music.
